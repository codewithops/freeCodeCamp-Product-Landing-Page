The Lip Bar

About

The Lip Bar was founded by Melissa Butler, who was motivated to create brightly colored lipstick for women of color. The Lip Bar is famous for their blue-based red liquid matte shade lipstick called "Bawse Lady", however, they have expanded into more beauty products. This includes lipsticks, skin tint, eye shadows, and recently skin care products. All of their products are vegan and celebrate diversity!

Melissa Butler, CEO of The Lip Bar
